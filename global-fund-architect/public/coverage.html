Status: Supported • Last verified: 2026-02-14
Informational only

Typical pathways (high-level)

  • EU-facing alternative funds (AIF): High-level mapping of AIF vs UCITS decision points; informational only. Passporting and marketing depend on AIFM/ManCo structure and investor location.

Marketing notes (high-level)

  • Cross-border marketing in the EU is governed by AIFMD/UCITS frameworks; implementation differs by Member State.
  • Always verify local implementation rules and any pre-marketing/marketing notification requirements.

  • EUR-Lex (Official EU Law Portal)
  • AIFMD (Directive 2011/61/EU) — 2011/61/EU
  • UCITS (Directive 2009/65/EC) — 2009/65/EC

Requires counsel when…

  • Any retail distribution considerations.
  • Any cross-border marketing / passporting decisions.
  • Any use of leverage, complex derivatives, or non-standard liquidity gates.
