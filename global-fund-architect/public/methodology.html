Methodology & Sources

GNCO is prototype software. Informational only. Not an offer. Not investment/legal/tax advice. Verify with qualified professionals.

Three-axis model

  • Fund domicile: where the fund/vehicle is established.
  • Manager domicile: where the manager/adviser is regulated.
  • Investor locations: where marketing / distribution occurs.

GNCO expresses outputs within this context and is not universal one-country advice.

Evidence hierarchy

  • Primary: regulator websites, official law portals, official gazettes.
  • Secondary: regulator guidance/Q&A, official circulars, official handbooks.
  • Not acceptable alone: blogs, marketing pages, unverified summaries.

Coverage statuses

  • Supported — curated entry with sources and reviewed triggers.
  • Partial — informational but requires counsel for many scenarios.
  • Planned — placeholder; do not rely without verification.

“Requires counsel” triggers

  • Retail distribution / public offering
  • Cross-border marketing and any passporting/NPPR decisions
  • Complex derivatives/leverage, unusual liquidity features, side letters
  • Delegation / sub-advisory / outsourcing across borders

Update policy (recommended)

  • Cadence: re-verify supported jurisdictions periodically and after major regulatory updates.
  • Traceability: each entry includes last_verified and primary sources.
  • Change log: record meaningful changes and reasons.
