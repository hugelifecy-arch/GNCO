What GNCO does

  • Provides a decision-tree workspace for structuring scenarios and documenting assumptions.
  • Shows coverage status per jurisdiction: Supported / Partial / Planned.
  • Links each jurisdiction to primary sources (regulators / official law portals).

What GNCO does NOT do

  • Not an investment product; no guaranteed returns; no APY/ROI.
  • Not legal, tax, accounting, or investment advice.
  • Not a substitute for qualified counsel/administrator review.

Responsible use

  • Always treat outputs as informational and validate for your fund/manager/investor context.
  • Use the Coverage Library to confirm what is supported today.
  • Where GNCO flags “requires counsel”, pause and escalate to qualified professionals.
  • Regulatory requirements vary by jurisdiction and change over time.
